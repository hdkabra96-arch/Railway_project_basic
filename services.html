Our Services

Track Work

We provide comprehensive track construction and maintenance services for all types of railway systems. From high-speed rail to heavy freight and urban light rail, our expertise ensures durable and precise track geometry.

  • Ballasted and ballastless track construction
  • Special trackwork and turnout installation
  • Continuous welded rail (CWR) laying
  • Track rehabilitation and emergency repairs

Electrification & Signaling

Powering the future of transit requires robust electrical infrastructure. We design, install, and test complex power and communication systems that keep trains moving safely and efficiently.

  • Overhead Catenary Systems (OCS)
  • Traction power substations
  • Positive Train Control (PTC) implementation
  • Wayside signaling and communications

Civil Engineering

Railway infrastructure extends far beyond the tracks. Our civil engineering division handles the heavy groundwork required to support modern transit corridors.

  • Earthworks, grading, and drainage
  • Bridge and tunnel construction
  • Retaining walls and sound barriers
  • Station and platform development
