About Us

Building the Future of Transit

Founded in 1998, Apex Railway Infrastructure has grown to become one of the nation's most trusted contractors for complex railway projects. We specialize in delivering end-to-end solutions for transit authorities, freight operators, and private developers.

Our team of over 500 engineers, project managers, and skilled tradespeople bring decades of specialized experience to every job site. We pride ourselves on our impeccable safety record and our ability to complete projects on time and within budget, even in the most challenging environments.

  • Sustainable Construction Practices
